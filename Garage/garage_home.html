Natural Language Quantification

Natural Language Quantification is the first product of ds4all.io. In a few seconds, it can read thousands of documents and infer a hidden structure describing all the documents. It permits to understand the patterns of any activity described by words and quantified by number. If you would like to know more, take a look at this short video:
