    Welcome to 'Data Science for all' ! Our mission is to empower people by provding a simple and free access to data science.

    Natural Language Quantification permits to: 1/ Find: patterns, topics, concepts in documents; 2/ Quantify: money, votes, views, likes generated by each topic; 3/ Assist you in the creation of high-impact documents. It can be applied to any activity described by words and quantified by numbers. NLQ is the fruit of a dynamic and flexible collaboration between indiviudals that sometimes do not even know each others. Thanks.
    Natural Language Quantification is the first product of ds4all.io: You can simply upload your .csv, submit the name of your file and get your report. Check the 'Tutorial' if you would like more information about how to perform this process. You can find some inspiration and examples of databases you can use (and get your first results in few minutes) if you look at the 'Projects'. "Everyone has the right freely to participate in the cultural life of the community, to enjoy the arts and to share in scientific advancement and its benefits." (Universal Declaration of Human Rights, Article 27). If you want to participate, click on 'Education': learn more about data science & get guidance to build your own algorithm (we start from scratch).
