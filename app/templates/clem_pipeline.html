About ds4all

The mission of 'Data Science for all' is to empower people by providing a simple and free access to data science. Data are everywhere and we believe that: "Everyone has the right freely to participate in the cultural life of the community, to enjoy the arts and to share in scientific advancement and its benefits." (Universal Declaration of Human Rights, Article 27). Data science is a huge scientific advancement and I am trying to do my part so that everyone can benefit from it. It starts by just being aware that data are around you and that you can benefit from it: You can draw from this field of information. Below are some examples of studies that I have performed; I found beauty in them. I hope you will too; for instance you can look at the plot on the bottom left corner, it's simple: when it gets cloudy people are renting bikes for shorter amount of time. Please let me know if you would like to know more about one of these stories.
